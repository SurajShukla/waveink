Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you deliver content?

Our turnaround times vary based on project scope and complexity. For standard blog posts and shorter content pieces, we typically deliver within 3-5 business days. For more complex projects like technical documentation or comprehensive content strategies, we'll provide a custom timeline during our initial consultation.

What are your payment terms?

For most projects, we require a 50% deposit upfront, with the remaining balance due upon project completion. For ongoing retainer relationships, we typically bill monthly. We accept payments via bank transfer and major credit cards.

Do you offer revisions on content?

Yes! All our content packages include up to two rounds of revisions to ensure you're completely satisfied. Additional revision rounds can be arranged at an hourly rate if needed. We're committed to delivering content that meets your expectations.

How do you handle content research?

Our process begins with thorough research. We dive into your industry, competitors, and target audience to ensure our content is accurate, relevant, and valuable. While we conduct our own research, we also welcome any materials or insights you can provide to help us better understand your business and goals.

Can you help with content distribution?

Absolutely! Beyond content creation, we offer content distribution strategy services to help maximize your content's reach and impact. This includes recommendations for channels, posting schedules, and optimization techniques to ensure your content reaches its intended audience.
